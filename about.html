About This Project

Student Tax Studio is a student-built educational project designed to help college students understand federal income tax basics.

Many students earn part-time income, freelance income, or internship pay but are unsure whether they need to file taxes or how refunds work. This tool simplifies that process.

This platform estimates federal income tax using standard deduction and bracket logic. It is not tax advice and does not replace professional services.

The goal is clarity, confidence, and better financial literacy among college students.

Educational tool only. Not tax advice.
