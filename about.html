About Student Tax Studio

Student Tax Studio is a student-built educational project designed to help college students better understand federal income taxes. It was created to simplify tax brackets, deductions, and filing requirements into plain language. This tool demonstrates how income and withholding interact under 2025 federal tax rules. It is not affiliated with the IRS and does not provide professional tax advice.

Educational tool only. Not tax advice.
