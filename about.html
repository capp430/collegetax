About This Project

Student Tax Studio is a student-built educational project designed to help college students better understand federal income tax responsibilities.

Many students earn income through part-time jobs, internships, or freelance work but feel unsure about filing requirements. This platform simplifies that process into understandable steps.

This project is not affiliated with the IRS and does not provide tax advice. It is an educational tool built to promote financial literacy and confidence.

Educational tool only. Not tax advice.
