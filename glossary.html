Glossary of Tax Terms

Filing Status

Your tax category.

Standard Deduction

Income excluded from taxation.

Taxable Income

Income remaining after deductions.

Withholding

Taxes already taken from paycheck.

Self-Employment Income

Freelance income subject to SE tax.

Marginal Tax Rate

Rate applied to last dollar earned.

How to Obtain Required Documents

W-2 from employer, 1099 for contract work, 1098-T from college.

How to File Your Taxes

Gather documents → Choose filing platform → Enter income → Review → Submit electronically.
