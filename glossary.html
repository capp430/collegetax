Tax Glossary & Filing Guide

W-2 Form

Shows wages earned and federal/state taxes withheld.

Standard Deduction

Fixed IRS amount reducing taxable income.

Taxable Income

Income remaining after deductions.

Withholding

Taxes already taken from paychecks.

State Income Taxes

States without income tax: Texas, Florida, Nevada, Washington, Wyoming, South Dakota, Alaska.

State taxes may require separate filing.

How To File

  1. Gather W-2 and 1099 forms.
  2. Use IRS Free File, TurboTax, or similar platform.
  3. Enter income accurately.
  4. Submit by April 15.
