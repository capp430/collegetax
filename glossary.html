Tax Glossary & Filing Guide

W-2 Form

A form your employer sends showing total wages earned and taxes withheld during the year.

Standard Deduction

A fixed amount the IRS allows you to subtract from income before calculating tax.

Taxable Income

The portion of income that is actually subject to tax after deductions.

States With Income Tax

Most states have income tax except: Texas, Florida, Nevada, Washington, Wyoming, South Dakota, Alaska.

How to File

Common filing platforms include IRS Free File, TurboTax, H&R Block, and FreeTaxUSA. Many offer free filing for simple returns.
