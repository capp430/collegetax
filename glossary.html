Tax Glossary & Filing Guide

W-2 Form

Issued by your employer showing wages earned and taxes withheld.

Standard Deduction

IRS fixed amount reducing taxable income before tax is calculated.

Taxable Income

Income remaining after deductions.

Withholding

Taxes already taken from your paycheck.

Dependent

If claimed by someone else, your deduction may change.

State Income Taxes

States with no income tax: Texas, Florida, Nevada, Washington, Wyoming, South Dakota, Alaska.

State taxes may require separate filing and affect your refund or balance.

How To File

  1. Gather W-2 and 1099 forms.
  2. Choose IRS Free File, FreeTaxUSA, TurboTax, or H&R Block.
  3. Enter income exactly as shown.
  4. Review deductions and credits.
  5. Submit electronically by April 15.
