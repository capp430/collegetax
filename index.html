Common Student Scenarios

Campus Job Only:
If you earned under the standard deduction and had taxes withheld, you may receive a refund.
Side Hustle (1099 Work):
Even if your income is low, you may owe self-employment tax.
Scholarships + Job:
Scholarships used for housing or non-qualified expenses may be taxable.

What Happens If You Don’t File?

- You could miss out on a refund.
- You may owe penalties if filing was required.
- Filing history may be needed for FAFSA, loans, or visa requirements.
